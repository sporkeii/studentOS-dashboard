## studentOS v2.0 🌸 <br>
hi!! so this is my project, studentOS. its basically a fake operating system that runs in your browser and its made for studying. i built the whole thing from scratch with just html, css, and javascript (no frameworks or anything, just pure code!!) <br>
i started making this because i was bored of normal study apps and wanted something that actually looked cool. it took me SO long but im really proud of how it turned out :) <br>

---

### what it looks like <br>
it looks like a real desktop!! there's a taskbar at the bottom, draggable windows, a start menu, everything. the theme is dark pink/neon by default but you can switch to light mode too. theres also little floating particles in the background which i think looks really pretty <br>

---

### all the apps 📱 :: <br>

**📚 student dashboard** <br>
this is like the main one. it shows all your subjects with progress bars, a to-do list, and a study streak tracker (its like the github contribution graph but for studying lol). you can: <br>

add subjects <br>
add tasks for each subject and tag them as PETA, notes, or other <br>
check off tasks and they get crossed out <br>
add general tasks too (like "EXAMS!!!") <br>
log study days and see your streak <br>

---

**📅 exam planner** <br>
this one i added because exams stress me out and i needed somewhere to track them. you can: <br>

add upcoming exams with a date and subject <br>
see how many days away each exam is (it turns red when its close!!) <br>
add study tasks for each specific exam <br>
track your progress per exam <br>

---

**🎵 music player <br>**
has 5 preloaded instrumental tracks that i actually listen to while studying. the album art spins when its playing which is my favorite detail. has full player / mini / background mode. theres also a mini player that stays at the bottom right corner so you can always see whats playing <br>

---

**⏰ study timer <br>**
pomodoro timer!! if you dont know what that is its basically: study 25 mins, break 5 mins, repeat. it has a circle ring that fills up as time passes and tracks which session youre on. you can also do short break (5 min) or long break (15 min) modes <br>

---

**📝 notes app <br>**
just a simple notepad basically. supports bold, italic, underline, and colored text. shows a live word count and you can download your notes as a .txt file. it doesnt autosave between sessions tho so heads up!! <br>

---

**🌌 3d interactive <br>**
this one is just for fun lol. its a 3d renderer i made from scratch (no libraries!!) that shows a spinning cube, sphere, or torus. you can move your mouse to tilt it and scroll to zoom. i spent way too long on this <br>

---

**⚙️ settings <br>**
you can change your display name, pick an avatar emoji (theres like 12 options including animals 🐱🦊), set a study status, and toggle dark/light mode. everything saves to your browser so it stays when you refresh <br>

---

**💡 guide <br>**
a built in tutorial that walks you through everything!! it shows up automatically the first time you open the app <br>

---

**keyboard shortcuts ⌨️ <br>**

space — play/pause music (only works when youre not typing) <br>
ctrl + → — next song <br>
ctrl + ← — previous song <br>

---

**stuff i built myself (no libraries!!) <br>**

the whole 3d renderer (cube, sphere, torus with rotation and projection math) <br>
the window drag system <br>
the streak heatmap grid <br>
the particle background <br>
the progress bar animations <br>
everything else too basically lol <br>

the only external things are google fonts (Orbitron, Share Tech Mono, VT323, Press Start 2P) and thats it! :) <br>
